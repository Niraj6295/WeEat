🌍 The Idea

Who We Are: We are a student-led initiative aiming to minimize food waste and feed the less fortunate within and around our campus. Our platform connects campus canteens, hostels, and restaurants with local NGOs, orphanages, old-age homes, and underprivileged families. Volunteers – mostly students and young professionals – help collect and deliver surplus food efficiently.

Our Goal: To create a self-sustaining, community-driven system where surplus food never goes to waste and everyone can contribute in their free time.

Our Vision: Simple and impactful: Reduce food wastage, fight hunger, and foster a culture of giving. We aim to establish autonomous campus chapters that manage their local food surplus while inspiring others to contribute, ensuring that surplus food is used to feed those who need it most.
