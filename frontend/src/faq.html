❓ FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

Q1. Who can donate food?
👉 Registered canteens, hostels, mess, restaurants, event organizers, or individuals with surplus food.

Q2. What kind of food can be donated?
👉 Freshly cooked, hygienic food following FSSAI guidelines. No expired, spoiled, or half-eaten food.

Q3. How do we ensure food safety?
👉 Donors declare preparation time and packaging condition. The system tags food as “Safe to Eat for X Hours”.

Q4. Why can’t students order too much food?
👉 To ensure fair distribution and avoid misuse, each student has a daily limit on the number of meals they can claim.

Q5. Why do NGOs get higher priority?
👉 NGOs serve vulnerable groups and communities; hence they are given bulk order rights and higher priority in receiving surplus food.

Q6. How is pickup/delivery managed?
👉 Pickup slots are scheduled via the app. Delivery is managed by volunteers, logistics teams, or NGO staff.

Q7. What happens if donated food isn’t claimed?
👉 Unclaimed food is diverted to compost/biogas plants, ensuring zero waste.

Q8. How does the platform track impact?
👉 The dashboard shows meals served, food saved, and carbon/water footprint avoided.
